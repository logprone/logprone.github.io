Things I use: ⌨️

Last updated: 2/01/2025

  • Phone Iphone 12 Mini This was the first phone that I bought with my own money. Small phone, Good build quality, Works well, Camera good, Battery life trash, will try to use it for 1 or 2 more years.
  • Laptop - Lenovo Ideapad S340 Bought this in 2020 still works great, upgraded the RAM to 12GB
  • Mouse - Logitech M330 very affordable general use mouse, can't go wrong with logitech. The only thing I want from my next mouse is blutooth connectivity as the receiver is another thing to carry and hogs another port. Will use this till it dies
  • Keyboard - Keychron K5 Pro This was my first mechanical keyboard and I got it in a banger deal in HK. Phenomenal Keyboard
  • Moniter - Samsung 27 inch IPS Moniter 75Hz Good enough moniter for day-to-day use.
  • Powerbank - Anker 322 MagGo This was bought because my phone doesnt have good enough battery life to go out without a Powerbank, and I got a great deal on it that too.
  • Earphones Wireless - Samsung Galaxy Buds 2 Very good sound profile, Small case, Good enough ANC that doesn't put pressure on my ears, Good enough battery life
