On Phone

  • Hevy - For Workout Tracking
  • Duolingo - For learning Japanese
  • HabitKit - Habit tracking app which puts the display as a github contributions graph
  • Google Tasks - Todo / List app
  • Notes - In-built notes app in Iphone
  • News on Air - I like how vividh bharati / Doordarshan news achors talk so I listen to news on this. It can also play local vividh bharati radio broadcasts.
  • Pocket Casts - Podcasts app
  • Pocket - save for later article reader
  • Headspace - Meditation app - Got free subscription from work.
  • Spotify - I listen to music on this because youtube music app sucks
  • lightroom - For editing photos
  • NTES - Train tracking
  • Bitwarden - Passwords Manager
  • Simple login - Random email generator tied to a proton mail account for random signups

On Computer

  • Chrome - Internet Browsing. I promise I will switch to a different browser in 2025
  • VScode - My choice of IDE
  • SumatraPDF - Very lightweight PDF reader, Installed this after I got sick of Adobe Reader updating way too frequently.
  • TreeSize - Very good app for Disk Management, can find files that hog storage, and is light on your Computer
  • Audacity - I dont use this much but when I have to do a bit of Audio editing this is fine.
  • PowerToys - A must have for windows users, Gives a spotlight like search feature, and makes the OS more usable in general
  • VLC media player - 10/10, no notes
  • Microsoft Word/Excel/Notepad - can't go without using these apps
